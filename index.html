Our principles

The following rules have always applied and will always apply to everyone, without exception.

Participants cannot pay to bypass the criteria. All advertisements must comply with the established standards.

We listen to our users. If an acceptable advertisement is reported by the community for a legitimate reason, it will be removed from the whitelist.

We can maintain our open source product free by charging large companies a fee for whitelist services. For smaller partners, these services may be offered free of charge.
